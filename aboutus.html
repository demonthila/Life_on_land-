ABOUT US

One touch of nature makes the whole world kin

OUR VISION

We want to make planting trees an easy way for anyone to help the environment. We can restore forests, generate biodiversity habitat, and have a beneficial social impact all around the world if we work together.

With every dollar donated, we plant a tree.

OUR MISSION

Protect, restore, and promote the sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems, manage forests sustainably, prevent desertification, and stop and reverse land degradation and biodiversity loss.

Our Process

You donate >> We pool >> Grow saplings >> Teams plant >> Monitored >> Your impact
